WELCOME TO ELYSEE FRIES AND BUSH BAR

Welcome to Elysee Fries and Bush Bar Hotel Ltd which is located in Awkuzu, a town in Nigeria that lies approximately 328km South of Abuja the country's capital city. There are three airports that are close to Abagana; Asaba International Airport, Akanu Ibiam International Airport(Enugu), Sam Mbakwe International Airport(Owerri). We offer executive services to our corporate and walk-in guest to enjoy the comfort and relaxation of their expectation about 1st star hotel. Our well equiped facilities and security provide sound atmosphere for our guests safety and memorable stay. Our Africa and English meal are excellently prepared to meet the taste and budget of every guest with variety of drinks from our world-class Garden and Resturant Bar.

Guests can order a variety of Nigerian staples in menu options at the onsite restaurant and enjoy an assortment of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks at the onsite bar.
